In 2021, I graduated from the University of Edinburgh with a 2:1 in BPhys. On my course, I studied primarily theoretical physics and programming using Python for simulation and data-modelling. After graduation, I decided to harness my interest and aptitude in coding and pivot towards a career in software/web development. While continuing to work in retail full-time, I started independently learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript; I've grown a lot since my very first webpage!

My most recent endeavour is to cement my qualifications as a programmer and software engineer through a HyperionDev bootcamp, where I've been filling in any gaps in my coding knowledge and learning best practice in SaaS.
